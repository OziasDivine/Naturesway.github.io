What To Know About Naturophathy

By restoring health to the body and enabling the body to achieve genuine health through a variety of modalities, naturopathic medicine targets the 1: underlying causes of symptoms and illness. 2: Alteration of Diet and Lifestyle 3: Botanical Medicine 4: Homeopathy 5: Physical Therapy 6: Counselling and Stress Management 7: customising clinical nutrition (vitamin, mineral, glandular supplementation) Instead of merely seeking to treat symptoms or dysfunctions by switching out pharmaceutical drugs for "natural agents," we aim for comprehensive disease eradication.

Every "protocol" is unique to the individual since treatment focuses on the patient, not the disease. The patient participates actively in their medical care and is expected to make the required lifestyle adjustments to achieve their recovery from illness. The main focus is on changing behavior, which entails making changes to one's diet, level of exercise, approach to managing stress, and quality of sleep. The side effects of naturopathic functional medicine are advantages that are considered to be a component of the outcome, i.e., when adopting a protocol with a focus on decreasing your cholesterol, your body fat percentage drops and your blood pressure decreases!
